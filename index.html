ABOUT THE FUND

SME Fund Kenya is a financial institution that offers affordable and flexible financing options to individuals and self-help groups in Kenya. Our loan website provides a convenient and efficient platform for individuals and self-help groups to apply for loans online.

With a range of loan packages, including personal loans, business loans, group loans, debt consolidation loans, payday loans, and auto loans, we aim to help our clients achieve their financial goals.

 

Our loan application process is easy and straightforward, consisting of 4 simple steps:

  • choose your loan package
  • enter your personal details
  • enter your business details (if applicable)
  • upload your documents.

How it works
Step 1: Choose your preferred loan package
To get started, simply browse our loan packages and choose the one that best suits your needs. We offer a range of loan packages, including personal loans, business loans, group loans
Step 2: Fill in personal details
We need to know a little bit about you in order to process your loan application. Enter your personal details, such as your name, contact information, and identification number. This information will be kept confidential and will only be used for loan application purposes.
Step 3: Fill in business details
Next you enter your business details. Ensure that the information you provide matches the details in the documentation. Applications with false or inconsistent details will be disqualified.
Step 4: Upload documents
To complete your loan application, you will need to upload certain documents, such as your identification documents, business registration documents, and financial statements. These documents will help us assess your loan application and make a loan offer. All documents will be kept confidential and secure.
